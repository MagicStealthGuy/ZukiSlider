Zuki Vikuli's Relationship Guide

0 Hearts: Zuki wants you dead. He doesn’t wish death on most ponies, so you must’ve actively pissed him off to reach this point.
1 Heart: Zuki hates you and will not be shy about letting you know. He will actively avoid or be aggressive with you at nearly every turn.
2 Hearts: Zuki dislikes you. He will actively ignore you, act condescending, and object to your thoughts.
3 Hearts: Zuki has a general disdain for you. He will be condescending towards you and not take you seriously.
4 Hearts: Zuki is slightly bothered by your behavior. He is more likely to sass you or criticize your actions.
5 Hearts: Neutral. Zuki will be more than polite with you as he is with everypony. He does not dislike you.
6 Hearts: Zuki considers you a friend. He will confide in you his personal feelings in good faith.
7 Hearts: Zuki really likes you. He will pray for your well being, trust your words, and participate in most activities involving you.
8 Hearts: Best Friends. At this point Zuki trusts you enough to be emotionally vulnerable with you. He willingly lets his guard down around you and accepts comfort instead of giving it.
9 Hearts: Zuki considers you his second family. He has an active and vested interest in your life and safety, and will actively fight tooth and hoof to protect you.
10 Hearts: Zuki will die for you. Your life in his eyes is as important as his mother’s life, his homeland, his princess. He shows a love and care for you so genuine that it could move mountains.
